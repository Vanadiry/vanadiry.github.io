Kirima Syaro

（Translated from Romaji, Official abbreviationシャロ）。

She is the debut character of Koi's comic "Is the Order a Rabbit?" and its derivative works.

15-year-old high school student (first appearance). She has golden curly hair and looks very temperamental on the outside, like a perfect lady.

Syaro’s name comes from Kilimanjaro Coffee（Kilimanjaro → キリマンジャロ → キリマ・シャロ → Kirima Syaro）。

Overview

Her financial situation is not good, she is careful every day and lives in poverty.
She likes crepes very much but she is afraid of spending money to buy them.

A little TSUNDERE and easily SHY.

She usually can't drink coffee because she will be as drunk as she is drinking, but she will not remember that memory.
Different coffees seem to have different effects.

She is very afraid of things such as haunting and courage test.
Because she was often chased by rabbits as a child, she was very afraid of rabbits.
To make matters worse, Yarn Road also has the physique to attract rabbits.

Strong computing power.

The clothes are mainly white, warm colors and blue. She often wears cute clothes with ribbons when she goes out.
